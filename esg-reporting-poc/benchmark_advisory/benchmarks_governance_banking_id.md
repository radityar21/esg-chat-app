## 1. ESG Governance Structure

| Aspek | BRI | BCA | DBS | Mandiri |
|-------|-----|-----|-----|---------|
| Dedicated ESG Committee | ✅ Risk Mgmt & ESG Committee (since 2021) | ESG Subdivision under Corporate Secretary | ✅ Board Sustainability Committee | ESG Task Force |
| ESG KPI linked to BoD Remuneration | ✅ Yes | ✅ Integrated to BoD & work unit KPIs | ✅ Yes (20% weight) | Partial |
| Reporting Line | Reports to President Director | Director of Planning & Finance → President Director | Chief Sustainability Officer → Board | Director → President Director |
| Frequency of Board ESG Review | Quarterly | ≥4x per year | Quarterly | Semi-annual |
| ESG Policy Decree | Circular of BoD (sustainability) | Multiple BoD Decrees (SLL, Green Loan, Sectoral ESG) | Board-approved ESG Framework | Internal policy |

**Referensi:** BRI ESG Committee established via Decision NOKEP 872-DIR/PPM/06/2022 [7][30][53]. BCA reviews quarterly with BoD [5][28].
