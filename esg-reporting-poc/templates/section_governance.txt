### EXACT TITLE (MANDATORY — DO NOT MODIFY)
title: "Climate Governance & ESG Oversight"

### OBJECTIVE
Generate a disclosure section describing the governance structures, processes, and
oversight mechanisms for climate-related risks and opportunities. This section
demonstrates board and management accountability for climate strategy.

### DATA INPUT
{
  "board_committee_name": "{board_committee_name}",
  "board_oversight_frequency": "{board_oversight_frequency}",
  "climate_expertise_present": {climate_expertise_present},
  "management_committee_name": "{management_committee_name}",
  "risk_committee_integration": {risk_committee_integration},
  "executive_remuneration_linked": {executive_remuneration_linked},
  "remuneration_climate_pct": {remuneration_climate_pct},
  "target_setting_authority": "{target_setting_authority}",
  "target_monitoring_frequency": "{target_monitoring_frequency}"
}

### RAG CONTEXT
{rag_context_governance}

### REQUIRED ELEMENTS
1. Board oversight paragraph:
   - Name of board committee responsible (e.g., Risk & Sustainability Committee)
   - Frequency of climate risk review (quarterly, semi-annual)
   - How climate considerations inform strategy and major transactions
2. Board competency statement:
   - Whether board members have climate expertise or training
   - How skills are assessed and developed
3. Management role paragraph:
   - Management-level position/committee responsible (e.g., Chief Risk Officer)
   - Reporting lines and escalation process
   - Integration with enterprise risk management
4. Target oversight:
   - Who sets climate targets (board vs management)
   - How progress is monitored and reported
5. Remuneration linkage (MANDATORY if executive_remuneration_linked = TRUE):
   - Percentage of executive compensation tied to climate metrics
   - Which metrics are used (e.g., emissions reduction, renewable energy %)
6. Governance structure diagram (OPTIONAL but recommended):
   - Visual representation of board → management → operational oversight

### FRAMEWORK-SPECIFIC NOTES
- GRI 2-12 / 2-13: Requires role of highest governance body in sustainability oversight
- IFRS S2 Para 5-7: Requires governance body identification + management role description
- CSRD ESRS 2 GOV-3: Requires integration of sustainability performance in incentive schemes
- OJK PSPK: Requires board-level sustainability committee for listed financial institutions

### CRITICAL CONSTRAINT
If executive_remuneration_linked = TRUE, remuneration_climate_pct MUST be disclosed.
If remuneration_climate_pct = 0 or NULL, state "not currently linked" explicitly.

### OUTPUT FORMAT
Return JSON per the contract in system_base.txt Section REQ-PROMPT-02.

    4. Generate specific recommendations to build governance data infrastructure
    5. Priority matrix: quick wins (e.g., board committee creation) vs structural changes
