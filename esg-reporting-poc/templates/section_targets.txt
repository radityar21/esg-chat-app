### REQUIRED ELEMENTS
1. Opening paragraph: state headline target (e.g., "50% reduction by 2030 vs 2020 baseline")
2. Target specification table:
   - Scope 1 target (absolute tCO2e)
   - Scope 2 target (absolute tCO2e)
   - Scope 3 target (absolute tCO2e or intensity-based)
   - Total reduction % vs base year
   - Target year and base year
3. Net zero commitment (INCLUDE if target_net_zero_year is not NULL):
   - Target year for net zero
   - Scope coverage (Scope 1+2 only, or all scopes)
   - Residual emissions strategy
4. SBTi validation statement (INCLUDE if target_validated_sbti = TRUE):
   - State that targets are validated by Science Based Targets initiative
   - Alignment with 1.5°C pathway
5. Interim milestones table:
   - 2025, 2027, 2030 targets (if applicable)
   - Progress status for each milestone
6. Reduction initiatives section:
   - List key initiatives (e.g., renewable energy procurement, energy efficiency, fleet electrification)
   - Expected contribution of each initiative (tCO2e reduction)
   - Implementation timeline
7. Progress assessment:
   - Current year emissions vs target trajectory
   - % progress toward 2030 target
   - On track / ahead / behind assessment
8. Decarbonization levers (MANDATORY for CSRD E1-4):
   - Own operations: energy efficiency, fuel switching, electrification, renewables
   - Value chain: supply chain engagement, financed emissions reduction
9. Carbon offset strategy (INCLUDE if carbon_offset_planned_use = TRUE):
   - Volume of offsets planned (tCO2e)
   - Type of offsets (nature-based, technological removal)
   - Certification scheme (e.g., Verra VCS, Gold Standard)
   - Permanence and additionality considerations
10. Renewable energy target (INCLUDE if renewable_energy_target_pct > 0):
    - Target % renewable energy by target year
    - Current % renewable energy
    - Procurement strategy (PPAs, RECs, on-site generation)

### FRAMEWORK-SPECIFIC NOTES
- GRI 305-5: Requires reductions in tCO2e, base year, scopes, methodology
- IFRS S2 Para 33-37: Requires quantitative targets, metrics, base period, milestones
- CSRD E1-4: Requires GHG reduction targets + decarbonization levers by scope
- OJK PSPK: Requires climate targets aligned with national NDC commitments

### CRITICAL CONSTRAINT
If target is NET (includes offsets), GROSS target MUST also be disclosed separately.
Offset volume MUST NOT exceed 20% of gross target per best practice (unless justified).
