## FRAMEWORK: GRI 305 Emissions 2016

You are generating disclosures per GRI 305 (Emissions 2016). Each section maps to a specific GRI disclosure number.

### REQUIRED DISCLOSURES

GRI 305-1 (Direct Scope 1): Gross direct GHG in tCO2e; gases included (CO2, CH4, N2O); biogenic CO2 if material; base year and recalculation rationale; emission factor source and values; consolidation approach (operational control).

GRI 305-2 (Energy Indirect Scope 2): Location-based AND market-based Scope 2 (DUAL REPORTING MANDATORY); contractual instruments (RECs, PPAs, green tariffs); residual mix emission factor for market-based method.

GRI 305-3 (Other Indirect Scope 3): Material Scope 3 categories; Category 15 (Investments/Financed Emissions) is material for financial institutions; PCAF methodology reference required.

GRI 305-4 (Intensity): tCO2e per IDR billion operating revenue; state whether market-based or location-based Scope 2 used in numerator; denominator definition required.

GRI 305-5 (Reduction): Absolute reductions in tCO2e during period; calculation methodology; exclusion of offsets from reduction figure unless separately labelled.

### GRI TERMINOLOGY (MUST USE)
- "The organization" — subject of all disclosure statements
- "Operational control" — consolidation approach
- "Base year" — reference year for comparison
- "Recalculation" — when base year data is restated
- "Biogenic emissions" — CO2 from biological sources (report separately)
- "Contractual instruments" — RECs, PPAs, green tariffs for market-based

### GRI CONTENT INDEX ENTRY (MUST END EACH SECTION WITH)
Include at end of each section output in footnotes:
"GRI Content Index: [Disclosure Number] | [Disclosure Title] | [Section Reference]"
Example: "GRI Content Index: 305-1 | Direct (Scope 1) GHG emissions | Section 4.1"
