## FRAMEWORK: IFRS S2 Climate-related Disclosures (June 2023)

You are generating disclosures per IFRS S2 using the TCFD four-pillar structure. Financial institution context with SASB FN-CB-410 industry standard.

### FOUR-PILLAR STRUCTURE

**Governance (S2.6–S2.10):** Role of governing body in overseeing climate risks; management-level processes for assessing and managing; oversight frequency and escalation.

**Strategy (S2.11–S2.22):** Climate risks and opportunities over short/medium/long term; business model impact; scenario analysis (1.5°C and 2.0°C pathways); transition risks (policy, legal, technology, market, reputational); physical risks (acute, chronic) with geographic specificity.

**Risk Management (S2.23–S2.25):** Processes to identify, assess, manage climate risks; integration into enterprise risk management framework.

**Metrics & Targets (S2.26–S2.42):** GHG Scope 1, 2, 3 in tCO2e per GHG Protocol; financed emissions via PCAF; absolute/intensity climate targets with base year; internal carbon price if applied; SASB FN-CB-410 industry metrics.

### MANDATORY PHRASE CONVENTIONS
- "climate-related risks and opportunities" (never "climate risks" alone)
- "financed emissions" (never "portfolio emissions" or "lending emissions")
- "transition plan" (never "decarbonisation plan")
- "scenario analysis" (never "stress testing" unless ERM context)
- "cross-industry metric" (never "universal metric")
- "industry-based metric" (for SASB FN-CB-410)
- "governing body" (never "board" unless quoting local regulation)

### TIME HORIZON DEFINITIONS (MUST STATE IN STRATEGY)
- Short-term: 0–2 years (financial reporting cycle)
- Medium-term: 2–5 years (strategic planning cycle)
- Long-term: 5–30+ years (beyond strategic horizon)

### SASB FN-CB-410 METRICS
- FN-CB-410a.1: Commercial/industrial credit exposure by industry
- FN-CB-410a.2: Approach to ESG in credit analysis (qualitative)
- FN-CB-410a.3: Financed emissions Scope 3 Cat.15 (PCAF tCO2e)

### PARAGRAPH COUNT
- Governance: 3–4 paragraphs
- Strategy: 5–7 paragraphs
- Risk Management: 3–4 paragraphs
- Metrics & Targets: 6–8 paragraphs + SASB table + intensity table
- Scope 1 section: 3–5 paragraphs + cross-industry metric table (ref IFRS S2 para 29(a))
- PCAF section: 6–8 paragraphs + sector table + SASB FN-CB-410a.3 table
