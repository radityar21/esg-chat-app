## FRAMEWORK: CSRD / ESRS E1 Climate Change (2023)

You are generating disclosures per European Sustainability Reporting Standards E1. All nine E1 datapoints must be addressed where data is available.

### REQUIRED DATAPOINTS (E1-1 through E1-9)

E1-1: Transition plan — Paris Agreement alignment; GHG reduction levers; CapEx/OpEx allocation; timeline with milestones; locked-in emissions.

E1-2: Policies — Policy scope (own operations + value chain); 1.5°C alignment; value chain engagement.

E1-3: Actions and resources — Specific actions taken/planned; resources allocated (IDR); expected outcomes; timeline.

E1-4: Targets — Absolute AND intensity targets; base year; target year; interim milestones; SBTi alignment status.

E1-5: Energy consumption and mix — Total energy (MWh); renewable vs non-renewable (%); fossil source breakdown.

E1-6: Gross Scopes 1, 2, 3 and Total GHG — By scope in tCO2e; by country if material; methodology; significant changes vs prior period.

E1-7: GHG removals and carbon credits — Removals (if any); credits purchased; retirement status; registry.

E1-8: Internal carbon pricing — Price (if applied); scope of application; decision-making influence.

E1-9: Anticipated financial effects — Physical risk exposure (IDR); transition risk exposure; opportunities; time horizons; % assets at risk.

### DOUBLE MATERIALITY STATEMENT (MUST INCLUDE)
"Climate change has been assessed as material under both impact materiality (the institution's impact on climate through GHG emissions) and financial materiality (climate-related risks and opportunities affecting the institution's financial position, performance, and cash flows)."

### ESRS-SPECIFIC TERMINOLOGY
- "Double materiality" — must appear in introduction
- "Impact, Risk, Opportunity (IRO)" — categorisation framework
- "Value chain" — specify: upstream, own operations, downstream
- "Transition plan" — ESRS E1-1; reference Paris Agreement
- "Locked-in emissions" — from existing assets/contracts
- "EU Taxonomy alignment" — if applicable; state % revenue/CapEx/OpEx
