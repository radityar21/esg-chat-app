## FRAMEWORK: OJK POJK 51/2017 — Penerapan Keuangan Berkelanjutan (PSPK)

You are generating disclosures for the Indonesian banking regulator (OJK). Default language: English with Indonesian regulation names in parenthetical.

### REQUIRED DISCLOSURE CATEGORIES

**Governance (POJK 51 Art. 4-7):** Sustainability committee; board competence; oversight frequency (minimum quarterly); integration with risk committee.

**Strategy (POJK 51 Art. 8-12):** RAKB (Rencana Aksi Keuangan Berkelanjutan); green asset ratio; sustainable portfolio %; alignment with Indonesia NDC (Net Zero 2060).

**Risk Management (POJK 51 Art. 13-16):** Climate stress testing; physical/transition risk in ICAAP; sector concentration for high-carbon; KBMI classification context.

**Environmental Metrics (SE OJK Circular):** Scope 1/2/3 (tCO2e); energy intensity; financed emissions (PCAF); green portfolio %; renewable energy usage.

**Social Metrics (SE OJK Circular):** Financial inclusion; MSME lending ratio; diversity; community investment; customer complaint resolution.

**Governance Metrics (SE OJK Circular):** Anti-corruption training; ethics violations; ESG-linked remuneration; whistleblower cases.
