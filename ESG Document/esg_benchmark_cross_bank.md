## 1. ENVIRONMENTAL — GHG Emissions & Energy

### 1.1 Scope 1, 2, 3 Disclosure (GRI 305-1 to 305-5)

| Best Practice | Bank Reference |
|---|---|
| Disclose Scope 1, 2, 3 emissions with YoY comparison (min 3 years) | **BRI** (pp.124-127), **OCBC** (Environmental Aspect table), **DBS** (GRI 305 assured) |
| Separate location-based vs market-based Scope 2 | **DBS** (dual reporting), **OCBC** |
| Include emission intensity ratio (tCO₂e/revenue) | **BRI** (GRI 305-4), **DBS** (GRI 305-4) |
| Report emission reduction initiatives & targets (GRI 305-5) | **BRI**, **BCA**, **OCBC** (carbon neutral operations Scope 1,2,3*) |
| PCAF Financed Emissions (Scope 3 Cat 15) with data quality score | **DBS** (NZBA aligned), **BRI** |

**OCBC Indonesia Key Metrics (2024):**
- Total Emissions: 20,609 tCO₂e (down from 21,398 in 2023)
- Energy consumption: 83,889 GJ
- Electricity: 23,144,925 kWh
- Maintaining carbon neutral in Scope 1, 2, and 3 (business trips)

## 8. REKOMENDASI UNTUK ESG REPORTING SYSTEM

Berdasarkan best practice dari seluruh bank di atas, berikut prioritas fitur yang harus ada di system:

### Priority 1 — Must Have (All banks do this)
- GHG Scope 1, 2, 3 disclosure with 3-year YoY → *Ref: BRI, DBS, OCBC, BCA*
- GRI Content Index generation (in accordance) → *Ref: All banks*
- Gender diversity metrics at multiple levels → *Ref: BCA, DBS, Mandiri, OCBC*
- Training hours per FTE with breakdown → *Ref: DBS, OCBC*
- Voluntary turnover rate → *Ref: BCA, OCBC*
- Anti-corruption & governance disclosures → *Ref: BRI, BCA, OCBC*

### Priority 2 — Differentiator (Leading banks)
- TCFD Content Index → *Ref: DBS, OCBC*
- SASB Index → *Ref: OCBC*
- External assurance marking per disclosure → *Ref: DBS*
- PCAF financed emissions with data quality score → *Ref: DBS*
- Sustainable finance portfolio breakdown → *Ref: OCBC*
- ESG Rating tracker → *Ref: DBS*

### Priority 3 — Emerging (Best-in-class)
- Carbon neutral operations claim with verification → *Ref: OCBC*
- Green Financial Crime awareness → *Ref: OCBC*
- Cyber resilience metrics (vulnerability rate) → *Ref: OCBC*
- Women SME financing tracking → *Ref: OCBC*
- Biodiversity impact metrics → *Ref: OCBC*
